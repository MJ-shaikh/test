Kelapur Toll Plaza

Nagpur – Maharashtra/Telangana Border Corridor

Kelapur Toll Plaza is situated on NH-44 forming part of an important four-lane National Highway corridor having a total project length of 138.15 kilometers.

The highway asset starts from south of Nagpur at Borkhedi (Km 36+600) and traverses through the districts of Nagpur, Wardha, and Yavatmal in the State of Maharashtra before ending near the Maharashtra–Telangana Border at Km 175+000.

The corridor includes two toll plazas namely Darodha Toll Plaza located at Km 92+500 and Kelapur Toll Plaza located at Km 150+450. The Kelapur Toll Plaza is proposed to be shifted to Km 156+650 for operational improvement and better traffic management.

The project supports efficient highway operation, advanced toll collection systems, ITS infrastructure, ATMS monitoring, CCTV surveillance, and safe traffic movement across the corridor.
