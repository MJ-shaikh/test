About NHIPMPL

Professional Highway Asset Management

NATIONAL HIGHWAYS INVIT PROJECT MANAGERS PRIVATE LIMITED (NHIPMPL) is a wholly owned entity of NHAI responsible for operation, monitoring, maintenance, and management of National Highway assets under NHIT and RIIT.

NHIPMPL ensures smart infrastructure management using modern technologies including ATMS, ITS, Toll Management Systems, surveillance systems, and advanced operational monitoring solutions across India's highway corridors.

The organization focuses on operational excellence, highway safety, intelligent transportation systems, project performance evaluation, and infrastructure sustainability.
