About NHIPMPL

National Highways InvIT Project Managers Private Limited (NHIPMPL) is a wholly owned entity of the National Highways Authority of India(NHAI) established for professional management, operation, maintenance, and monitoring of highway assets under NHIT and Rajmarg Infra Investment Trust frameworks.

NHIPMPL manages Toll Management Systems (TMS), Advanced Traffic Management Systems (ATMS), FASTag ecosystem, centralized surveillance systems, digital highway monitoring, and integrated infrastructure operations across multiple National Highway corridors. The organization supports intelligent toll operations, operational analytics, highway monitoring, digital infrastructure management, and real-time technology-driven operational solutions across India.
