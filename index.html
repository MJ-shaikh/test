Welcome to NHIPMPL

NATIONAL HIGHWAYS INVIT PROJECT MANAGERS PRIVATE LIMITED (NHIPMPL) is a wholly owned entity of NHAI responsible for operation, management, maintenance, monitoring and performance supervision of National Highway assets under NHIT and RIIT. NHIPMPL ensures advanced infrastructure management through Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS), Advanced Traffic Management Systems (ATMS), Toll Management Systems (TMS), highway surveillance, electronic toll collection, road safety, and modern operational excellence.
