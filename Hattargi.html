Hattargi Toll Plaza

Belgaum – Kagal Highway Corridor

Hattargi Toll Plaza is part of the Belgaum – Kagal National Highway corridor located in Karnataka. The corridor is an important highway route supporting commercial transportation, passenger movement, and regional connectivity.

The toll plaza is equipped with intelligent toll collection systems, FASTag operations, CCTV surveillance, traffic monitoring systems, and integrated highway operational infrastructure.

Project Details

Asset Group: NHIT R1

Location: Karnataka

Operated By: NHIT

Operation Type: Toll Operations & Highway Asset Management
