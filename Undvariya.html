Undvariya Toll Plaza

Abu Road – Swaroopganj

This road is adjacent to the Palanpur – Abu Road (Asset 1) and starts near Abu Road in Rajasthan at km 646+000 and ends at km 677+000 near Swaroopganj in the state of Rajasthan. The asset has one toll plaza at Undavariya (km 670+750). Abu Road – Swaroopganj has similar traffic pattern and connectivity as Palanpur – Abu Road (Asset – 1).

The project supports efficient highway operation, toll collection management, ITS systems monitoring, and safe traffic movement across the corridor.

Project Details

Asset Group: NHIT R1

Location: Rajasthan

OPerated By: NHIT

Operation Type: Toll Operations & Highway Asset Management

Systems: ETC, WIM, ATMS, CCTV Surveillance, Traffic Monitoring
