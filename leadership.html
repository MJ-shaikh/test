Leadership Team

Sh. Sunil Yadav

Chief Executive Officer & Director

Shri Sunil Yadav, CEO & Director, has more than 36 years of distinguished experience in the planning, execution, and management of large-scale infrastructure projects, including National Highways, roads & buildings. Out of which nearly 19 years in National Highways Authority of India. Before taking charge of CEO & Director of NHIPMPL, he worked as Regional Officer, National Highways Authority of India, Jaipur and Gandhinagar, Director of SPVs of NHAI, Chief Operating Officer (COO) National Highways Logistic Management Limited (NHLML). He has done B. Tech. in Civil Engineering from Bangalore University and has attended various training programmes on “Contract Management, Arbitration & Dispute Resolution” at Indian Academy of Highway Engineers (IAHE), Management Development Programme on Arbitration, National Institute of Financial Management and International Study Tour on “Ropeway Systems for Public Transportation”, Paris & Toulouse, France sponsored by Asian Development Bank & French Development Agency. He has been awarded the NH Excellence Award 2020 and has also received Appreciation Letters from State Government for active support of NHAI.

Sh. Ashish Kumar Singh

Director

Sh. Ashish Kumar Singh has around 20 years of experience in Public Finance. He also possesses in depth experience relating to various infra project finance, New Chart of Accounts, Performance Review Management System and Electronic Revenue Accounting package etc. He started his career from September 2002 as Civil Servant in (2002 Batch). Over the last 20 years, he has been in asset management of finance sector including Roads, Buildings, Budgeting Expenditure Monitoring, Technical Issues etc. At present, he is holding the post of CGM (F) in NHAI as well as Director in NHIPMPL. Ashish Kumar Singh is an ICAS, 2002 (IAS Examination 2001) Batch graduate in Bachelor of Arts (Hons.) from Lucknow University of History, Political Science, and Anthropology. MBA in Finance from National Institute of Finance Management, Faridabad and Post graduate Diploma in year 2007-08 in Development Studies from “Institute of Developing Economies Advance School”, Japan External Trade Organization (JETRO), Chiba, Japan. He has served as CAO in Uttar Pradesh Rakiya Nirman Nigam (UPRNN), as Deputy CGA in Ministry of Finance, CA/CCA in CBDT, Petroleum Chemical Fertilizer, National Pharmaceutical in Govt. of India before join as CGM (F), NHAI in November 2022.

Sh. Ajay Batra

Head – Projects & Operations

Shri Ajay Batra is the Head – Projects and Operations at National Highways InvIT Project Managers Private Ltd. He comes with over three decades of experience in the Highway Construction industry, after serving across varied terrains in various parts of the country. In his varied leadership roles in various Government Organizations, he has successfully overseen the planning, design and execution of roads and highways, bridges, ROBs, EUPs /VUPs, optimizing asset performance and building high-quality, resilient infrastructures. He carries a thorough understanding of building and maintaining high-value road assets that supports economic growth and safer mobility for all users. As Head Projects & Operations, he leads project delivery, maintenance and Toll Operations of highway projects while ensuring optimal performance and safety of existing assets over their lifecycle.
